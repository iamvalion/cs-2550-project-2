Interests

I enjoy learning about cybersecurity and software engineering, both in school and as a hobby. They are fascinating fields because I enjoy building things and immersing myself in the ever-changing and expanding digital world we live in. I very naturally gravitated toward this career path. In the past few years I have become more focused on digital security and privacy, as they need to be taken seriously when technology is evolving at such a rapid pace.

I also am very interested in music. It's captivating, transcends cultural barriers, and connects people. I listen to a ridiculous amount of music, and I hope to eventually produce some of my own. Lately I was able to reconnect with some old friends, and we've been having jam sessions where we play our various instruments, do a bit of song writing, and dabble with mixing and mastering.

Last year I started rock climbing, specifically bouldering. Bouldering is type of climbing with a focus on shorter routes. This requires a lot of strength, technique, and problem-solving; at the same time, it requires very little gear, which I like. It has been a great experience for me, as I have been able to improve myself physically, mentally, socially, and even spiritually. This has happened as I've trained very hard, strategically overcome mental barriers, connected with many lovely people, and spent more time in nature (though most of my climbing is indoor).
